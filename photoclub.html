Here at the Photography Club, we enjoy all types of photography: landscape, cityscape, nightscape, portrait, product, street, long exposure, tilt-shift, ...anything!

Throughout our club sessions, we go around Tokyo to take photos, and then in other sessions we edit them back at school.

Please look forward to the Culture Festival, where we will have a photo booth!
